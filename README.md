# King County House Sales Dashboard

Interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing housing sales trends in King County, Washington over 120+ years (1900-2020).

**[View Live Dashboard on Tableau Public](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/jessica.singh2877/viz/KingCountyHouseSales_17064129810660/KingCountyHouseSales?publish=yes)**

## Overview
This project visualizes housing market trends in King County, WA, enabling users to explore price patterns, geographic distributions, and property characteristics through interactive filters and visualizations.

## Key Features
- **Time Series Analysis**: Average house sales prices over 120+ years
- **Interactive Filters**: Month, year built, square footage (living and lot)
- **Geographic Heat Map**: Sales distribution across King County neighborhoods
- **Condition Analysis**: Price variations by house condition and view quality
- **Property Distributions**: Bathroom and bedroom frequency analysis

## Insights
- Historical price trends show significant variations over decades
- House condition and view quality strongly correlate with pricing
- Geographic patterns reveal neighborhood-level market differences
- Property characteristics (bedrooms, bathrooms) follow predictable distributions
